1. Introduction

Skeeter's Mobile Oil Service ("Skeeter's," "we," "us," or "our") uses Smartcar's API to access certain vehicle data with your explicit consent. This Privacy Policy explains what vehicle data we access through Smartcar, how we use it, how it is protected, and the choices you have regarding your data. By connecting your vehicle through Smartcar Connect, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data We Collect

Through Smartcar, and only with your explicit authorization, we may access the following categories of information from your vehicle:

  • Vehicle signals — including odometer reading, oil life percentage, oil temperature, and transmission temperature, and other data points relevant to effective preventative maintenance.
  • Vehicle attributes — including make, model, and Vehicle Identification Number (VIN).

We only request the specific data permissions that are configured in our Smartcar dashboard. We do not request, and Smartcar will not grant us, access to any data category beyond what is explicitly configured and authorized.

3. How We Use the Data

Vehicle data accessed through Smartcar is used solely to support the maintenance services we provide, including to:

  • Display your vehicle's current status and service-relevant readings.
  • Optimize and personalize your preventative maintenance scheduling.
  • Track mileage for fleet management purposes, where applicable.
  • Maintain accurate service history and support proactive customer communication.

4. Data Sharing

We will never sell your vehicle data. Your data is processed securely and is only shared with third parties when required by law or in response to valid legal process, such as a subpoena, court order, or other proper court documentation. Outside of these limited circumstances, your vehicle data is not disclosed, traded, or shared with any third party.

5. Consent and Revocation

You grant us access to your vehicle data by authorizing the connection through Smartcar Connect. This authorization is explicit and specific to the data permissions configured in our Smartcar dashboard.

You may revoke this access at any time. Revocation can be completed through your Smartcar-connected account settings, or by contacting us directly using the information below. Once access is revoked, we will no longer be able to retrieve new vehicle data through Smartcar.

6. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect the vehicle data we access and store. Access to this data is limited to authorized Skeeter's personnel, and we take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
