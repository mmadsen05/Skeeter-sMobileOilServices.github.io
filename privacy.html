1. What Information We Collect

Through Smartcar, and only with your explicit authorization, we may access the following categories of information from your vehicle: • Vehicle signals — including odometer reading, oil life percentage, oil temperature, and transmission temperature, and other data points relevant to effective preventative maintenance. • Vehicle attributes — including make, model, and Vehicle Identification Number (VIN). We only request the specific data permissions that are configured in our Smartcar dashboard. We do not request, and Smartcar will not grant us, access to any data category beyond what is explicitly configured and authorized.

2. How We Use Information

Vehicle data accessed through Smartcar is used solely to support the maintenance services we provide, including to: • Display your vehicle's current status and service-relevant readings. • Optimize and personalize your preventative maintenance scheduling. • Track mileage for fleet management purposes, where applicable. • Maintain accurate service history and support proactive customer communication.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We will never sell your vehicle data. Your data is processed securely and is only shared with third parties when required by law or in response to valid legal process, such as a subpoena, court order, or other proper court documentation. Outside of these limited circumstances, your vehicle data is not disclosed, traded, or shared with any third party.
